Privacy Policy for Catholic Prayers HRDM

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Catholic Prayers HRDM (“the App”) is a devotional application designed to provide users with access to Catholic prayers, including the Rosary and Divine Mercy Chaplet. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we do — and do not — collect.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Catholic Prayers HRDM does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage data
  • Diagnostics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

The App functions entirely offline and does not transmit any data to external servers.

2. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage
  • Social media integrations
  • SDKs or tracking libraries

No data is shared with any external party because no data is collected.

3. Children’s Privacy

Because the App collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

4. Security

Since the App does not collect or store any information, there is no personal data at risk. All functionality occurs locally on the user’s device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
